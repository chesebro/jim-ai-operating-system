# Strategic Narrative Framework

Use this framework when shaping positioning, category language, story, or market-facing messaging.

## Questions to answer

1. What is this?
2. Who is it for?
3. What problem does it solve?
4. Why does it matter now?
5. What makes it different?
6. What is the clearest way to explain it?
7. What should the audience remember?

## Output structure

- What this is
- Who it is for
- Problem and stakes
- Why now
- Differentiation
- Clear narrative
- Memorable takeaway
