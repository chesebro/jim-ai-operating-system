# Opportunity Framing Skill

Use this skill when exploring a new idea, venture, consulting opportunity, or product concept.

## Output Structure

1. Core problem
What real problem exists and who experiences it?

2. Target user or buyer
Who cares enough about this problem to act?

3. Existing alternatives
How do people solve this today?

4. Why now
Why might this be newly possible or newly valuable?

5. Wedge
What small entry point could make this viable?

6. Risks and assumptions
What must be true for this to work?

7. Next experiment
What is the fastest way to test whether this idea has real potential?
